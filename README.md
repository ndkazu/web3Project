The School Of Men Dao
A mental health care platform that allows:

Users to access formular giving them a profile of their mental state (free)
User to have consultations with veted professionals, and even customised consultations courses with different professionals, specially tailored to match their psychological profile.
Users to have access to the platform community to not only share about their concerns, but also participate to a governance process in order to influence decisions concerning the platforn development
professionals to have access to a secured and updated database compiling the latest societal trends in term of mental health by countries/gender/financial situation
Platform Mechanisms
Roles

User: Access the different services offered by the platform in exchange for basic personal information, and/or more for an additionnal fee. Participation to platform community discussion and governance (Basic track) for paying user's.

Professional: Provide mental evaluation formular, online consultation services, consultation curriculum adapted to the user's profile. Participation to platform governance (basic track)

Scientific Council: Selection of platform professionals, and additionnal concil members through governance system (Council track) and involved in other sensitive decisions concerning

Governance System

Two types of tracks: Basic Track: Accessible by paying user's role and above. participants can make proposals concerning addition of some specific workshops, or organisation of events for the community Council Track: Only for council members. addition of council members, professionnals, and also expenses are decided there.

Subscriptions

User's subscription:

Free: Users need to provide some basic information, and be registered into the database, in order to gain access to the Formulars.
Paying: In order to access additionnal services, a subscription payment is necessary.
Council & Professional subscriptions: Accessible through request by a paying user to the council. the council will then start a ballot to finalize and deliver it's decision concerning the request.

Technology
Smart-contract: Ink!
To manage user roles, subscriptions, and automated actions in general

Polkadot-sdk: an Open-Gov Pallet
A pallet that will implement a customised open-governance system adapted to the needs of the platform. A trait will be implemented to allow an easy access to open-gov (pallet-governance + pallet-conviction) and customization from a single pallet. Packing the whole open-gov in one single pallet, makes it also easier to access from an ink! smart-contract.

Indexer: Subsquid
For large user database management, an indexer like subsquid makes sense.
